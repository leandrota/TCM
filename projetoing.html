Maurício De Sousa Institute

The Maurício de Sousa Institute has several support programs, campaigns, social actions and projects. Per believe in a better future and to help future generations.

Check out some of these projects below:

Vôlei Mania is a social sports project with the aim of assisting children and teenagers in area of social vulnerability providing citizenship values. The initiative also has one of strengths of the volleyball base category in the State and the Country.

The ONE FOR ALL AND ALL FOR ONE Program! FOR ETHICS AND CITIZENSHIP is an initiative of CGU in partnership with the Maurício de Sousa Institute. The Program is part of a set of training and knowledge management, aimed at children and young people, known as “Citizen Education”. The UPT aims to propose learning experiences related to ethics and citizenship, awakening the interest of students and the participation of the entire school community in the development of actions, thus providing a collaborative teaching-learning process. The school is the main nucleus for developing UPT actions, but the proposed themes aim the preparation of students for effective performance in the contexts of which they are part.

The Dodói Project aims to promote the well-being of hospitalized children with cancer, prepare them emotionally for treatment, promote integration with the care team nursing and the medical staff and, mainly, with the hospital environment so that, in addition to easing their suffering and insecurity, lead them to a faster and more comfortable recovery. Addresses clearly and objective, in a way that is typical of childhood, the concepts of lymphoma, leukemia, bone marrow transplantation It is their treatment phases, facilitating the sick child, in a playful way, to express and understand in your needs, whether physical or psychological.

The project consists of collecting seals from aluminum cans, selling them to a company what recycles the material and reverses the value obtained from the purchase of wheelchairs, benefiting people and institutions with people with total, partial, temporary or permanent disabilities, children, young people, adults or elderly people. To purchase a chair, you need 140 bottles (2 liters) of seals, which correspond to approximately 80 kg of aluminum.
